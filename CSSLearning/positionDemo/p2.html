Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the India national cricket team. He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League as a right-handed batsman. He is often considered one of the best batsmen of his era and some critics believe him to be one of the best limited-overs batsmen in history.[3] Between 2013 and 2022, he captained the India cricket team in more than[specify] 200 matches across all three formats. With 40 wins out of 68 test matches, Kohli is the most successful test captain in Indian cricket history.[4] Kohli made his Test debut in 2011.[5] He reached the number one spot in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen for the first time in 2013.[6] He has won Man of the Tournament twice at the ICC World Twenty20 (in 2014 and 2016). He also holds the world record of being the fastest to 23,000 international runs.[7]
